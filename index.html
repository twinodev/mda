About Us

MDA (Mbarara Dance Academy) is a youth-driven dance academy based in Mbarara, Uganda. Founded by Sizzy Afro, the academy focuses on Afro dance, street dance, and contemporary urban styles. We believe dance is a powerful mode of communication, expression, and empowerment.

What We Do

Dance Training

Structured training programs for beginners and advanced dancers focused on skill, discipline, and creativity.

Event Performances

High-energy performances for corporate events, schools, weddings, festivals, and brand activations.

Youth Empowerment

We nurture confidence, teamwork, and leadership through dance and creative expression.
