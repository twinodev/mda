About Us

MDA (Mbarara Dance Academy) is a youth-driven dance academy based in Mbarara, Uganda. Founded by Sizzy Afro, the academy focuses on Afro dance, street dance, and contemporary urban styles. We believe dance is a powerful mode of communication, expression, and empowerment.
